<p><a href="https://www.faasafety.gov/wings/pub/learn_more.aspx" target="_blank">WINGS</a> is an FAA program of ongoing learning and proficiency.  Completing WINGS phases can reset your flight review clock.</p>
<p>The most important things you will want to do are:</p>
<ul>
    <li>(Optional) Complete an approved WINGS activity and log it in a flight entry</li>
    <li>(Optional) Have your instructor sign that flight entry</li>
    <li><span class="b">(REQUIRED)</span> Ask your instructor to register that activity on the <a target="_blank" href="https://www.faasafety.gov/cfi/pub/cfiactivitygivecredit_1.aspx">FAA Wings</a> website.  This is the official record of your WINGS progress.  You can use the link below to make this request.</li>
    <li><span class="b">(REQUIRED)</span> If your activity completed a phase, denote that fact by attaching the &quot;WINGS Phase Completed&quot; property to the flight entry where you completed the phase.  This will tell %APP_NAME% to reset your flight review due date.</li>
</ul>
<p>Here's a helpful tip for WINGS activities: in the &quot;WINGS Activity Completed&quot; property for a flight, press the open-backet key (&quot;[&quot;) and start typing the Activity ID for an activity or some keywords from it, and you can choose from a list of known activities.  You can add multiple activities to the field this way.</p>

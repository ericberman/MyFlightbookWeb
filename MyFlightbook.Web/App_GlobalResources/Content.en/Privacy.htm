Choices

Account management

You can log in and click on ’Profile’ to update or delete your account. Check out our FAQ for more information.

If you choose to delete your account you will receive an emailed copy of your logbook data (just in case), but we delete all of your other information, with two exceptions:

  • If you are an instructor and have signed flights for a student, your name/email are preserved on the student's record. We do NOT email that email address; rather, it serves as a unique identifier to say that the signer had that email on the date of signing.
  • For development and support purposes, we do keep backup copies of the database going back a few months. Usually, only the most recent of these backups is installed on a development machine. Deleting your account does not affect database backups from prior to the deletion.

Please note that if you set up a relationship with an Instructor, the Instructor will have the ability to issue digital endorsements to you and to view (but not edit/delete) endorsements. You can revoke the ability of an instructor to view your logbook at any time. You can also delete the relationship; doing so will prevent them from accessing your account in any way, but will preserve any signatures or endorsements that they provided during the tenure of the relationship. You can, of course, also re-establish the relationship at any time.
